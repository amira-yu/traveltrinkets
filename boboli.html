Boboli Gardens

Florence, Italy

Trinket Type: Ticket

Location of collection: 43°46'06.7"N 11°14'59.8"E

Year collected: 2025, February

Extra information: A nice garden walk with plenty of different statues from multiple eras, each one has a plaque in front of it so you can learn more about the specific piece. I was able to get insight into the statues while still enjoying the scenery, there is also a nice view once you reach the top of the garden.
