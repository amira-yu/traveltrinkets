Asakusa Tourist Center

Japanese character which says "flower"

Japanese character which says "star"

Tokyo, Japan

Trinket Type: Paper/card, hand written

Location of collection: 35°42'38.5"N 139°47'47.8"E

Year collected: 2024, December

Extra information: The Asakusa Tourist Centre is a multi-storey building that provides extra unique information and history regarding this tourist attraction. Located across the street, it provides a break from the inescapable crowds surrounding the historic site itself, along with a free observation deck on the 8th floor for wonderful, elevated views. The next floor down, they were running a small free workshop for writing Japanese characters when I was there. This same floor had many images high quality images and facts from throughout the years about Asakusa, although if my memory serves me right, they were mostly in Japanese.
