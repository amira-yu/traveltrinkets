The guidelines about the Tokyo Subway Ticket Guidelines reads as follows and is from December 2024:

  • With this ticket, you are able to ride any Tokyo Metro or Toei Subway line.
  • Please use the automatic ticket gates to enter or exit the subway.
  • 24-hr. Pass: Good for 24 hours of unlimited rides from the time the pass is first used in the automatic ticket gates within the valid period.
  • 48-hr. Pass: Good for 48 hours of unlimited rides from the time the pass is first used in the automatic ticket gates within the valid period.
  • 72-hr. Pass: Good for 72 hours of unlimited rides from the time the pass is first used in the automatic ticket gates within the valid period.
  • If you enter the ticket gates within the valid hours and the pass expires while you are riding the subway, you will still be able to exit.
  • Unused tickets will become invalid upon expiry of the valid period.
  • Please ask at the place of purchase for details regarding refund eligibility and the refund process.
  • You may use this ticket on the Tokyo Metro (Ginza Line, Marunouchi Line, Hibiya Line, Tozai Line, Chiyoda Line, Yurakucho Line, Hanzomon Line, Namboku Line, & Fukutoshin Line) and on the Toei Subway (Asakusa Line, Mita Line, Shinjuku Line & Oedo Line.) You cannot use this ticket on JR lines (Yamanote Line etc.) or on private railway lines.
